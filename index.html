Ship30 AI Residency is a 30-day, outcome-driven program for solo founders, indie hackers, and early-stage AI builders who want to break through noise and ship real products. Hosted in carefully selected, distraction-free locations, the residency blends structured deep work, peer accountability, expert mentorship, and local exploration. No equity, no fluff, just one intense month to build, launch, and grow something meaningful.

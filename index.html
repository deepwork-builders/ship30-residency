About the Residency

Ship30 AI Residency is a 30-day, outcome-driven program for solo founders, indie hackers, and early-stage AI builders who want to break through noise and ship real products. Hosted in carefully selected, distraction-free locations, the residency blends structured deep work, peer accountability, expert mentorship, and local exploration. No equity, no fluff—just one intense month to build, launch, and grow something meaningful.

FAQs

Is this only for solo founders?

Primary program is solo / duo, but we run Micro‑Team Sprints; inquire for dates.

Do you take equity?

No. We're a pay‑and‑ship residency; your cap table stays clean.

Food preferences?

Veg & non‑veg counters; Jain meals available on request.

Internet reliability?

Dual 300 Mbps fibre lines, automatic 4G fail‑over, 4‑h UPS; uptime ≥ 99.5 %.

Can I arrive late or leave early?

We discourage it—cadence matters. Early departures forfeit bond.

Refund if I cancel?

75 % refund ≥ 30 days before start; 25 % ≥ 14 days; bond non‑refundable < 14 days.
